Weekly Meeting Format

Every meeting follows the same four-point agenda: simple, efficient, and focused.

Standard Agenda: Every Meeting

1. What did you do this week?

Each researcher reports measurable progress.

2. What worked?

Highlight successful methods, tools, or insights.

3. What broke?

Discuss blockers, errors, or failed analyses.

4. Next week’s goal

Define one clear, deliverable-oriented objective.
